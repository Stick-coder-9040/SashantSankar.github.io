About Me

I am passionate about both music and electronics, always seeking new ways to blend creativity with technology. From building Arduino-based projects like smart glasses and modular robots to exploring advanced sensor systems, I enjoy pushing the boundaries of what DIY engineering can achieve.

In addition to my work with electronics, I have a strong connection to music production. I create original compositions, design MIDI controllers, and experiment with different digital audio workstations to develop a unique sound. Music, much like engineering, allows me to combine structure with imagination.

I believe in continuous learning and multidisciplinary exploration. Whether I am writing code, designing hardware, or producing a new track, I strive to bring a professional, thoughtful approach to every project.
